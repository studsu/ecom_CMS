Shipping Policy

We are committed to delivering your order accurately, in good condition, and always on time.

  • All orders are shipped within 1–2 business days of confirmation.
  • Metro cities: Estimated delivery time is 2–4 working days.
  • Tier 2 & 3 cities: Estimated delivery in 4–7 working days.
  • Remote and rural areas: May take up to 10 working days.

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. Please note that delivery timelines may vary based on courier partners and external factors like weather and holidays.

For any shipping-related queries, please contact our support team. We’re here to help.
